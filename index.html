Navigation

I am a computer scientist with the Air Force Research Lab's Information Directorate. I research artificial intelligence and machine learning with an emphasis on collective and emergent beahviors, multi-agent teaming and task allocation, and ad hoc teaming.

I completed my master's degree at Brigham Young University in Computer Science in 2013 and graduated with honors with a Bachelor's degree also from Brigham Young University in Mathematics in 2011.

Since graduating I have been employed at AFRL, but will soon be pursuing a PhD at UT Austin!
